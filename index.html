TL;DR

  • Researchers counted 5.63 apparent injuries per team per match in international football. Only 0.41 were real — about 7%.
  • The NBA fines floppers up to $30,000. The NHL fines embellishment. England's FA bans divers retroactively. FIFA does almost nothing.
  • Football has already proven punishment works: the one player FIFA banned for life for faking an injury never did it again.
  • The fix: retroactive video review, escalating bans, treatment means you leave the pitch — and a stopped clock, so flopping stops paying.
